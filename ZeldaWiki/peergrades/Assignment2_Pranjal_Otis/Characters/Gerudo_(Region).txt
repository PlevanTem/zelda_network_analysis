{{Term/Store|Gerudo||Series, BotW, HWAoC}} is a recurring {{Term|Series|Region|link}} in {{TLoZ|series}}.<ref>{{Cite|Gerudo|Map|BotW}}</ref>{{Name Ref|HWAoC}}

==Features and Overview==
==={{BotW|-}}===
The Gerudo region is made up of the [[Gerudo Desert]] and the [[Gerudo Highlands]]. It is home of the [[Gerudo]] people and the villainous [[Yiga]] Clan. It contains four [[Molduga]]s and nine [[Stone Talus]]es.

====Gerudo Desert====
{{Main|Gerudo Desert}}
The Gerudo Desert make up the southern portion of the Gerudo Region. It contains [[Gerudo Town]], [[Kara Kara Bazaar]], the [[Great Fairy]] [[Tera]] and the {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Naboris|link}}.

====Gerudo Highlands====
{{Main|Gerudo Highlands}}
The Gerudo Highlands make up the northern and eastern portion of the Gerudo Region. It contains the [[Yiga Clan Hideout]], the [[Gerudo Tower]] and the [[Wasteland Tower]].
