==Features and Overview==
{{Term|BotW|Dueling Peaks South}} is located along the southern slope of the {{Term|BotW|Dueling Peaks|link}} in {{Term|BotW|West Necluda|link}}. There is a collection of {{Plural|BotW|Ore Deposit|link}}, including a {{Term|BotW|Rare Ore Deposit|link}}, in the crevice north of {{Term|BotW|Dueling Peaks South}}, directly east of {{Term|BotW|Shee Vaneer Shrine|link}}.

The {{Term|BotW|Stone Talus (Junior)|link}} can be found here.
