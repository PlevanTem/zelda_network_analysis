{{Term/Store|Central Hyrule||Series, BotW, HWAoC}} is a recurring {{Term|Series|Region|link}} in {{TLoZ|series}}.<ref>{{Cite|'''{{Big|Central Hyrule}}'''|Map|BotW}}</ref>{{Name Ref|HWAoC}}

==Features and Overview==
==={{BotW|-}}===
{{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule}} is the central {{Term|BotW|Region}} of {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}}, containing the {{Term|BotW|Great Plateau|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Hyrule Field|link}} and the {{Term|BotW|Hyrule Ridge|link}}. {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} begins his quest in this {{Term|BotW|Region}} after waking up in the {{Term|BotW|Shrine of Resurrection|link}} on the {{Term|BotW|Great Plateau}}. At the northern edge of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule}} lies {{Term|BotW|Hyrule Castle (Breath of the Wild)|link|display= Hyrule Castle}}, which holds {{Term|BotW|Calamity Ganon|link}}.

It is known, during the time of {{Term|BotW|Calamity Ganon}}, as one of the most dangerous {{Plural|BotW|Region}} in {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}}, due to vast amounts of {{Term|BotW|Guardian|link|display= Guardians}} being present there and myriad monsters, such as {{Plural|BotW|Bokoblin|link}}.

====Great Hyrule Forest====
{{Main|Great Hyrule Forest}}
The Great Hyrule Forest is a large wooded area that covers the vast majority of northern Central Hyrule,<ref>{{Cite Book|quote='''{{Big|Central Hyrule}}''' |book= CaC |publisher= Dark Horse Books |page= 221}}</ref> consisting of smaller woods that together make up the entire area. The [[Lost Woods]] is the largest of all its forests, which surrounds the [[Korok Forest]] in its core, and is itself surrounded by a deep gorge known as [[Lake Mekar]]. The Great Hyrule Forest further extends north toward the {{Term|BotW|Eldin Mountains|link}}, ending at the isolated [[Thyphlo Ruins]].

====Great Plateau====
{{Main|Great Plateau}}

The {{Term|BotW|Great Plateau}}, in the southwest part of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule}}, is the subregion of said area where {{Term|BotW|Link}} begins his quest and leaves to journey the rest of {{Term|BotW|Hyrule}}.

====Hyrule Field====
{{Main|Hyrule Field}}

{{Term|BotW|Hyrule Field}} is the subregion making up the majority of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule}}, and a good amount of {{Term|BotW|Hyrule}} in general. It contains many monsters and {{Plural|BotW|Guardian|link}}, as well as {{Term|BotW|Hyrule Castle (Breath of the Wild)|link|display= Hyrule Castle}}.

====Hyrule Ridge====
{{Main|Hyrule Ridge}}

{{Term|BotW|Hyrule Ridge}}, in the northwest part of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule|link}}, is a subregion with a rockier terrain and higher elevated areas, with the the major part of it consisting of the {{Term|BotW|Thundra Plateau|link}}.
