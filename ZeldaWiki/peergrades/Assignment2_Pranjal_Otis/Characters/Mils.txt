{{Term/Store|Mils||Series, BotW}} is a character in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|Mils|N/A|BotW}}</ref>

==Biography==
Mils is a [[Hylian]] who hunts for treasure with his sister [[Mina]]<ref>{{Cite|That's where we come in. We're treasure hunters, searching for a one-in-a-million payday.|Mina|BotW}}</ref>. The siblings can be found near the [[Exchange Ruins]] north of the [[Great Plateau]]. The two of them can be attacked by [[Bokoblins]].<ref>{{Cite|I knew it wasn't smart to go treasure hunting in these parts.|Mils|BotW}}</ref>

Mils wants to go home but his sister demands that they keep searching for treasure.<ref>{{Cite|But my sister says this is where we search, then this is where we search. Even if I wish we could just go home.|Mils|BotW}}</ref>
