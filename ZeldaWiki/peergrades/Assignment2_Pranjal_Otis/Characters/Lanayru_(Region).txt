{{Term/Store|Lanayru||Series, BotW, HWAoC}} is a recurring {{Term|Series|Region|link}} in {{TLoZ|series}}.<ref>{{Cite|Lanayru|Map|BotW}}</ref>{{Name Ref|HWAoC}}

==Features and Overview==
==={{BotW|-}}===
{{Term|BotW|Lanayru (Region)}} is an eastern {{Term|BotW|Region}} of the {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}}, containing the {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Great Spring|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Sea|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Wetlands|link}}, and {{Term|BotW|Mount Lanayru|link}}. The {{Term|BotW|Region}} is the primary source of {{Term|BotW|Hyrule}}'s freshwater and is home to the aquatic {{Term|BotW|Zora|link}} people.<ref>{{Cite Book|quote= |book= CaC |publisher= |page= 273}}</ref> It is bordered by the {{Term|BotW|Akkala|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Eldin (Region)|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule|link}}, and {{Term|BotW|Necluda|link}} {{Plural|BotW|Region}}.

====Lanayru Great Spring====
{{Main|Lanayru Great Spring}}
The {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Great Spring}}, located in the central part of {{Term|BotW|Lanayru (Region)}}, consists of an elevated plateau and several noteworthy {{Term|BotW|Water (Terrain)|link}} features. It holds areas such as {{Term|BotW|Zora's Domain|link}} and {{Term|BotW|East Reservoir Lake|link}}, the latter of which contains {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Ruta|link}}. Numerous waterfalls dot the area and form rivers that flow into the {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Wetlands}}, lending to an increased amount of {{Term|BotW|Lizalfos|link}}.

====Lanayru Sea====
{{Main|Lanayru Sea}}
The {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Sea}}, located in the easternmost part of {{Term|BotW|Lanayru (Region)}}, is a large body of {{Term|BotW|Water (Terrain)}} adjacent to the steep, rocky cliff faces of {{Term|BotW|Tal Tal Peak|link}}. It primarily contains {{Term|BotW|Spool Bight|link}} along the region's coastline.

====Lanayru Wetlands====
{{Main|Lanayru Wetlands}}
The {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Wetlands}} are a relatively flat, western sub-{{Term|BotW|Region}} bordering {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule|link}}. The shallow marshes have several islands connected by crude, makeshift bridges. The area holds the {{Term|BotW|Goponga Village Ruins|link}} and is occupied by a fair amount of monsters.

====Mount Lanayru====
{{Main|Mount Lanayru}}
{{Term|BotW|Mount Lanayru}} is a notably tall, cold mountain in the southeastern part of {{Term|BotW|Lanayru (Region)}}. It borders the {{Term|BotW|Necluda|link}} {{Term|BotW|Region}} and contains the {{Term|BotW|Spring of Wisdom|link}} near its peak. At the peak of {{Term|BotW|Mount Lanayru}}, {{Term|BotW|Naydra|link}} can be found, taken over by {{Term|BotW|Malice|link}}.

====Shatterback Point====
{{Main|Shatterback Point}}
{{Term|BotW|Shatterback Point}} is a cliff at the summit of {{Term|BotW|Ploymus Mountain|link}}.
