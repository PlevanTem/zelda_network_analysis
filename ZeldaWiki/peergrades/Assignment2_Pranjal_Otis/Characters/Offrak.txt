{{Term/Store|Offrak||Series, BotW}} is a character in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|Offrak|N/A|BotW}}</ref>

==Biography==
Offrak is a [[Goron]] child who can be found rolling around [[Goron City]]. Offrak observes that since Link is a [[Hylian]], he needs [[Fireproof Elixir]]s to withstand the heat.<ref>{{Cite|A {{Color|BotW Blue|Hylian}} like you needs this, right? It's a {{Color|BotW Blue|fireproof elixir}}!|Offrak|BotW}}</ref> He offers to sell him one for 60 [[Rupee]]s.<ref>{{Cite|Here's what I'll do, fella. Just for you, I'll sell ya that {{Color|BotW Blue|fireproof elixir}} at a special price of only {{Color|BotW Red|60 rupees}}!|Offrak|BotW}}</ref> Offrak is business savvy,<ref>{{Cite|<small>{{Color|BotW Gray|Heheh... It all worked out...}}</small> Er, nothin'!|Offrak|BotW}}</ref><ref>{{Cite|A sale! I actually made a sale!! Here ya go, brother!|Offrak|BotW}}</ref> and gets very upset if Link refuses his offer.<ref>{{Cite|Well, don't come cryin' to me when you're on fire, fella!|Offrak|BotW}}</ref>

During the night, Offrak can be found at the [[Protein Palace]], falling asleep late at night. He can be woken up to buy a Fireproof Elixir.<ref>{{Cite|Hmm hm hmmmm... Whoa! An actual customer?!|Offrak|BotW}}</ref>
