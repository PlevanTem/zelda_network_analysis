{{Term/Store|Kachoo||Series, BotW}} is a character in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|Kachoo|N/A|BotW}}</ref>

==Biography==
Kachoo is a [[Gerudo]] who manages the [[Inn]] at the [[Kara Kara Bazaar]]. She is upset that the Inn has been losing business ever since the {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Naboris|link}} showed up in the [[Gerudo Desert]].<ref>{{Cite|Grrr... We've lost a lot of business ever since that {{Color|BotW Blue|Divine Beast}} started stomping out in the desert.|Kachoo|BotW}}</ref> Kachoo assures [[Link]] that while the [[Divine Beast]] may be loud, the beds at the Inn are still comfy.<ref>{{Cite|The sound of the Divine Beast walking might be loud, but the beds are still comfy. Do you want to stay?|Kachoo|BotW}}</ref> If Link speaks to Kachoo while wearing the [[Gerudo Set]], she says that the clothes look nice on him.<ref>{{Cite|You know, those clothes look quite nice on you. Even on close examination, you look every bit the fine vai.|Kachoo|BotW}}</ref> After Link calms Naboris, Kachoo says that people can sleep soundly at the Inn now without the constant stomping.<ref>{{Cite|With the Divine Beast gone, you can sleep soundly without being woken up by constant stomping. Want to stay?|Kachoo|BotW}}</ref>
