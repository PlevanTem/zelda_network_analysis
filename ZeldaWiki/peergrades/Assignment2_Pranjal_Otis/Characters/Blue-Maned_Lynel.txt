{{Term/Store|Blue-Maned Lynel|Blue-Maned Lynels|Series, BotW|plural}} are recurring {{Plural|BotW|Enemy|link}} in {{TLoZ|Series}}.<ref>{{Cite|'''{{Color|BotW Shrine Quest|Blue-Maned Lynel}}'''|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref>

==Characteristics==
==={{BotW|-}}===
In ''Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild'', Blue-Maned Lynels have 3000HP, and can be found carrying the [[Mighty_Lynel_Sword|Mighty Lynel Sword]], [[Mighty_Lynel_Spear|Mighty Lynel Spear]], or [[Mighty_Lynel_Crusher|Mighty Lynel Crusher]]. Additionally, Blue-Maned Lynels always carry a [[Mighty_Lynel_Bow|Mighty Lynel Bow]]. When defeated, Blue-Maned Lynels will drop either [[Ice_Arrow|Ice Arrows]], [[Fire Arrow|Fire Arrows]], or [[Shock_Arrow|Shock Arrows]]. Depending on game progression (which affects the strength of enemies you encounter, as well as the weapons they carry), Blue-Maned Lynels will appear in different places across Hyrule.
{{Compendium/Extract|Blue-Maned Lynel}}
{{Term|BotW|Blue-Maned Lynel}} are the blue-colored variant of the red-colored {{Plural|BotW|Lynel|link}}. They have been in {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}} since ancient times.<ref>{{Cite|These fearsome monsters have lived in Hyrule since ancient times.|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref> Like other {{Plural|BotW|Lynel}}, they will often refrain from attacking for a short period of time after spotting {{Term|BotW|Link}}, instead opting to stare down its opponent. Being a higher tier variant from red-colored {{Plural|BotW|Lynel}}, they carry "Mighty" set of {{Term|BotW|Lynel Gear|link}}, such as a {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Sword|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Shield|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Bow|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Spear|link}}, and a {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Crusher|link}}. In {{Term|BotW|Master Mode|link}}, they carry the weaker {{Term|BotW|Lynel Gear}} set.
