==Biography==
{{Lacking Sources|BotW}}
{{Term|BotW|Pondo}} can be found just outside of {{Term|BotW|Pondo's Lodge|link}} in {{Term|BotW|Hebra|link}} patting down a {{Term|BotW|Snowball|link}}. If {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} talks with him, he will ask if {{Term|BotW|Link}} would like to try a round of {{Term|BotW|Snowball Bowling|link}} for 20 {{Plural|BotW|Rupee|link}}. If {{Term|BotW|Link}} bowls a strike, {{Term|BotW|Pondo}} will reward him a {{Term|BotW|Blizzard Rod|link}}. After the first strike, {{Term|BotW|Pondo}} will give {{Term|BotW|Link}} 300 {{Plural|BotW|Rupee}} for each subsequent strike.
