{{Term/Store|Mount Lanayru||Series, BotW}} is a location in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|'''Mount Lanayru'''|Map|BotW}}</ref>

==Features and Overview==
{{Term|BotW|Mount Lanayru}} is a large mountain within the {{Term|BotW|Lanayru Range|link}}, south of {{Term|BotW|Zora's Domain}}. It is considered a sacred site and is home to the {{Term|BotW|Spring of Wisdom|link}} at its peak. The site is off-limits to anyone under the age of 17, lacking the requisite wisdom to be there.<ref>{{Cite Book|quote=Mount Lanayru is an extraordinarily cold mountain to the south of Zora's Domain. This sacred place, named for the Goddess of Wisdom, Nayru, is off limits to anyone under the age of seventeen since they lack the prerequisite wisdom to be there. The Spring of Wisdom sits at the top.|book= CaC|publisher= |page= 283}}</ref> {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} must journey to {{Term|BotW|Mount Lanayru}} as part of the {{Term|BotW|Shrine Quest|link}} "{{Term|BotW|The Spring of Wisdom|link}}". Upon reaching the summit, {{Term|BotW|Link}} will find {{Term|BotW|Naydra|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Spring of Wisdom}}'s guardian, possessed by {{Term|BotW|Malice|link}}.<ref>{{Cite|You found no treasure but instead discovered Naydra, the Spring of Wisdom's guardian spirit, who has been overtaken by darkness.<br>Break through the evil force possessing Naydra to set this great spirit free!|The Spring of Wisdom|BotW}}</ref> When {{Term|BotW|Naydra}} is freed, {{Term|BotW|Link}} recieves {{Term|BotW|Naydra's Scale|link}}, which is used to reveal the {{Term|BotW|Jitan Sa'mi Shrine|link}}. Due to its cold nature, {{Term|BotW|Link}} will constantly take damage if he does not wear any cold-resistant {{Term|BotW|Armor|link}}, such as the {{Term|BotW|Snowquill Set|link}}, or eats cold-resistant {{Plural|BotW|Food|link}}, like {{Plural|BotW|Spicy Pepper|link}}.

Three {{Plural|BotW|Korok|link}} can be found near {{Term|BotW|Mount Lanayru}}'s peak. The first two can be uncovered by melting ice blocks to the northwest and the third {{Term|BotW|Korok}} is to the northeast where a circle of rocks must be completed.
