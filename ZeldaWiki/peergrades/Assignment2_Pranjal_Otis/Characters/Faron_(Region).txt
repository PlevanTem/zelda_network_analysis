==Features and Overview==
==={{BotW|-}}===
{{Term|BotW|Faron (Region)}} is a {{Term|BotW|Region}} in the southeast of {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}}. It is a jungle-like {{Term|BotW|Region|link}} home to {{Term|BotW|Lurelin Village|link}} and many beaches. Unlike most other major {{Plural|BotW|Region}}, {{Term|BotW|Faron (Region)}} is not home to any {{Plural|BotW|Divine Beast|link}}.

In the deep jungle there are many {{Plural|BotW|Fruit}}, ancient stone landmarks, and at the coasts of the {{Term|BotW|Region}} there are beaches. 

Lots of {{Plural|Series|Animal|link}}, {{Plural|BotW|Fruit}}, and {{Plural|BotW|Plant|link}} live here, like {{Plural|BotW|Hearty Durian|link}} and {{Term|BotW|Mighty Bananas|link}}.
