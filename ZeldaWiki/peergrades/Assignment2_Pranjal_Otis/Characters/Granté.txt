{{Term/Store|Granté||Series, BotW}} is a character in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|Granté|N/A|BotW}}</ref>

==Biography==
{{Lacking Sources}}
{{Term|BotW|Granté}} is a half-{{Term|BotW|Hylian|link}}, half-{{Term|BotW|Sheikah|link}} merchant.{{Fact|BotW}} He is the son of the researchers {{Term|BotW|Robbie|link}} and {{Term|BotW|Jerrin|link}}.<ref name="Diary"/> On the day his father suggested he should explore the outside world, {{Term|BotW|Granté}} left his family home, the {{Term|BotW|Akkala Ancient Tech Lab|link}}, and traveled the world whilst studying {{Term|BotW|Armor|link}} before settling in {{Term|BotW|Tarrey Town|link}}. He appears in {{Term|BotW|Tarrey Town}} after the "{{Term|BotW|From the Ground Up|link}}" {{Term|BotW|Side Quest|link}} is complete and will sell {{Plural|BotW|Armor Set|link}} to {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} that have no other method of being replaced after being sold, such as Expansion Pass {{Plural|BotW|Armor Set}} or those found within {{Plural|BotW|Ancient Shrine|link}}. He will also sell {{Term|BotW|Link}} a new {{Term|BotW|Hylian Shield|link}} for 3,000 {{Plural|BotW|Rupee|link}} if the original is lost or broken.
