{{Term/Store|Silver Moblin|Silver Moblins|Series, BotW|plural}} are recurring {{Plural|Series|Enemy|link}} in {{TLoZ|series}}.<ref>{{Cite|'''{{Color|BotW Shrine Quest|Silver Moblin}}'''|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref>{{Name Ref|HWAoC}}

==Characteristics==
==={{BotW|-}}===
{{Compendium/Extract|Silver Moblin}}
{{Plural|BotW|Silver Moblin}} are tall monsters that can be found all over {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}}. Due to {{Term|BotW|Calamity Ganon|link}}'s magic, they are a powerful variant of {{Term|BotW|Moblin|link}}, surpassing even {{Plural|BotW|Black Moblin|link}}, but are outclassed by [[Golden Moblin]]s.<ref>{{Cite|The strongest of all Moblins, Ganon's fiendish magic has allowed them to surpass even the Black Moblins in strength and resilience.|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref> They are armed with the strongest {{Term|BotW|Moblin}} {{Plural|BotW|Weapon|link}}.{{Which}} When defeated, they may drop {{Plural|BotW|Moblin Horn|link}}, {{Plural|BotW|Moblin Fang|link}}, {{Plural|BotW|Moblin Guts|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Amber|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Opal|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Topaz|link}}, {{Plural|BotW|Ruby|link}}, {{Plural|BotW|Sapphire|link}}, and {{Plural|BotW|Diamond|link}}.

==Other Appearances==
==={{HWAoC|-}}===
{{Noncanon|Start}}
Silver Moblins reappear in {{HWAoC}}, first appearing in [[Relentless as a Waterfall]]. During Pulse of the Ancients DLC Missions, as well as EX [[Scenario]]s, some can be seen wielding containers with [[Bomb Barrel]]s during battle. A variant that utilizes [[Sky Octorok]]s is also introduced during the EX Scenarios.
{{Noncanon|End}}
