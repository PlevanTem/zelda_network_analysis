==Characteristics==
{{Compendium/Extract|Guardian Scout IV}}
The Guardian Scout IV is the strongest variation of the {{Term|BotW|Guardian Scout|link}}, found within "A Major Test of Strength" {{Plural|BotW|Ancient Shrine|link}}. In order to complete these shrines, {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} must defeat the Guardian Scouts IV inside. Being the most upgraded versions, Guardian Scouts IV are capable of wielding three weapons at once.<ref>{{Cite|This model is very resilient and has been outfitted with triple-blade functionality, allowing it to wield three weapons. This will put any would-be hero to the test for sure.|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref> They are equipped with any combination of a {{Term|BotW|Guardian Sword++|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Guardian Shield++|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Guardian Spear++|link}}, or {{Term|BotW|Ancient Battle Axe++|link}}.
