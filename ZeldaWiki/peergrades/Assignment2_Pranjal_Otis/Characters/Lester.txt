==Biography==

If spoken to, he will request that {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} bring him Goron spice,<ref>{{Cite|If you happen to get your hands on some {{Color|BotW Blue|Goron spice}}, can you give it to me?|Lester|BotW}}</ref> which begins the "[[Curry for What Ails You]]" {{Term|BotW|Side Quest|link}}. He will reward Link with 50 {{Plural|BotW|Rupee|link}} upon receiving the spice.<ref>{{Cite|Hey...
